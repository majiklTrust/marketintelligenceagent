What it does

Built for people who have something to say.

Three responsibilities, handled continuously, so you can focus on the work that makes you worth reading in the first place.

◉

Autonomous research

Ingests trusted RSS sources around the clock, tracks breaking developments across the topics you care about, and ranks what matters by corroboration across independent reporting.

◈

Grounded drafts

Writes posts in your voice, cited to real sources, checked for factual alignment and tone. Never pulled out of thin air, never published without a paper trail back to the original reporting.

◆

Human in the loop

Nothing ships without your approval. Review, edit, or reject every draft before it hits your feed. Set the cadence, keep the final word.

How it works

Three steps from signal to post.

  1. 01

    Pick your topics

    Tell the agent what you want to follow. Cybersecurity, AI policy, industry shifts — your call. Point it at the feeds you already trust.

  2. 02

    The agent researches and drafts

    It reads the feeds, identifies what matters, corroborates across sources, and writes posts in your voice with citations attached.

  3. 03

    Approve and publish

    Review the queue on your schedule. Publish with one click, or let the cadence controller handle the timing for you.
